About Us

Our Mission

Our mission is to revolutionize the way readers discover and engage with sequential art. By focusing on characters as the driving force behind our content and enabling readers to select stories based on their favorite characters, rather than traditional titles, we aim to create an unparalleled, personalized reading experience that caters to the unique interests and passions of each individual.

"I got out of my car and followed the suspicious entity around the building. I lost sight of it so I returned to my car and drove home. As I'm walking into my house I hear my trunk pop open. I turn around only to witness the entity exit my trunk, close it and skate backwards out of my driveway with flouresent blue rollerblades fitting firmly on its' feet. It pointed in my direction and continued eyeballing me until it was out of my line of sight. After witnessing this I couldn't help but think...Where does one find flourescent blue rollerblades at nowadays?"

Dude That's Missing, "Unfinished Journal of Dude That's Missing" - Year, Doesn't Matter
