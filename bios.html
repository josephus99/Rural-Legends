Character Bios

Featured Bios: Peter from Kentucky. If you push him to his limit...his hands will be transformed into hooves and he will push back...WITH A VENGENCE.

It seems to be an ordinary day in Louisville. The people are smiling and going about their day as planned. Peter is down by the Ohio River stealing flowers from the city garden. Peter enjoys being at the brown colored Ohio River because it is very peaceful there. He revels in the absence of condescending eyes that allows him to practice his country line dancing in peace, without being ridiculed as the only male child in a family whose father constantly reminds him of how he wants a real son and not a want-a-be MerMan boy. This particular morning Peter feels that something is different. He can’t figure out what it is. He starts to walk around the lake looking to see something that he has not seen before. He then spots a beautiful red flower. “This is not a rose.” Peter thinks to himself as he plucks the flower from the ground.He smells the flower and is amazed at how good the flower smells. It has a scent like and apple. Peter is blown away by the strong apple scent so he sniffs the flower continuously for about thirty-five minutes. Peter then begins to feel really good. He starts to reason with himself saying, “If this flower smells like an apple there is a possibility that it may taste like an apple.” He begins to eat the flower. And to his surprise it does taste like an apple. He begins to eat more and more of the flowers. “This is going to be my lunch today.” he says to himself while munching down on the beautiful red apple-tasting flowers. The gardener who is assigned to watch the city garden spots Peter snatching the red flowers from the ground and stuffing the flowers into his mouth. . Before long Peter has eaten the entire flowerbed. The gardener quietly creeps up on Peter...

Peter continues to anonymously help those in distress not settle for less. He is the unwilling hero that the world can't do without. He may show up if you need him. He may not show up if you need. Only he knows. He relies on the hairs on his knuckles to direct his heart, fists and his eyes in the right direction. His fist will protect you or reject you depending on your attitude. He aims to right the wrongs for the right people. If you wrong him once he'll let you know twice that you chose the right man to wrong at the right time. Actually he'll let you know twice to the 2nd power....Starting with his left fist and completing the cycle with his right fist.
